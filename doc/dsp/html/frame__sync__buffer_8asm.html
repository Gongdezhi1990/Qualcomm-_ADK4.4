[SDK Folder]/src/kalimba/frame_sync/frame_sync_buffer.asm

Part of ADK_CSR867x.WIN. 4.4

Called at start of frame. Should be fixed at frame period (7.5 msec) Check for available data/space Zero Pad or Overlap
